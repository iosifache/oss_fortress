Ubuntu Portrait

The small brother of Ubuntu Landscape

Ubuntu Portrait is a lightweight piece of software installed on your Ubuntu server that allows you to control it from the comfort of your browser. The beta release allows you to examine your files using the commands you're already familiar with and upload new ones directly into your home directory. Other features will be added in future versions, so stay tuned!
